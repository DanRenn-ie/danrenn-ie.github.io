As A Lighting Programmer

I am an experienced programmer on a wide variety of consoles ranging from smaller more mobile desks such as Chamsys MQ70, Zero88 FLX S24 and Avolites Titan mobile to larger more proficient desks such as the Avolites TigerTouch or Grand MA2 full size. This wide range of console layouts and softwares gives me a very versatile and adaptable process when programming and lets me quickly adapt to new consoles and softwares with ease.

I also have experience working with individual pixel control using grids and pixel mapping on various consoles from Avolites to MA3

I am able to program blind, with a visualizer and in a live setting with both a timecode or busking mindset. this makes me very versatile in many situations and lets me quickly adapt to the needs of the event or performance. I love working with clients and artists throughout both the design and programming phase communicating and brainstorming with them to create the best lightshow that we can and have it fit the intended event perfectly.
